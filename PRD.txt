Neuro-Symbolic FIR Assistance System (NSFAS)
1. Vision Statement
To develop an AI-powered decision-support system that assists police officers in drafting legally accurate,
explainable, and structured FIRs by combining Natural Language Understanding with Symbolic Legal
Reasoning.
The system will not replace human judgment. Instead, it will act as an intelligent legal assistant that reduces
drafting effort, improves consistency, and provides explainable recommendations for applicable IPC/BNS
sections.
2. Problem Statement
Current FIR drafting faces several challenges:
Manual Interpretation
Officers must manually interpret victim statements and identify applicable legal sections.
Knowledge Dependency
Accuracy often depends on the experience and legal knowledge of the officer .
Time Consumption
Complex complaints require significant analysis before drafting.
Lack of Standardization
Similar incidents may be classified differently across officers.
Explainability Gap
Existing AI text-generation systems can generate content but often fail to justify why a legal section applies.
